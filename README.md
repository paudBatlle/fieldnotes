# fieldnotes
Repo to store all the projects done in my way to discover stuff

## Info
After finishing my internship at SWISS as a Data Analyst, I realized I have taken many roles and worked on many different projects in the last years, from traditional ML such as time series forecasting and NLP, to LLM focused systems including chatbots, RAG and agents, to Data Science and Data Engineering.

I've really enjoyed all these projects and I like switching — being multidisciplinary and working across industries, from healthcare to aviation, from marketing to tech consulting.

I feel like I have touched a lot of branches but only on the surface.

After some careful reflection about what I like to do, how I work and where I am going, I divided my interests into three personalities:

**Scholar:** Drawn to fundamentals, papers, and the kind of understanding that takes time. Prone to rabbit holes. Happiest when re-deriving something from scratch or asking why something actually works.

**Scientist:** Thinks end-to-end. Cares about the full pipeline from messy data to a decision someone can act on. Likes connecting technical rigor to real-world meaning.

**Engineer:** Systems-focused. Thinks about scale, failure modes, and production. Wants to build things that hold up.

Most of the time all three show up at once. But naming them helps.

Now that I have some time, I want to dive deeper into each one and explore them — to discover what I actually like, not just what I have happened to work on. That is why I created this repo, to document my journey and share my progress. Some weeks will produce clean notebooks. Others will produce more questions than answers. Both count.
